Description

The training system is a web that is used to manage the existing training system in a company. This system belongs to PT. Citra Langgeng Sentosa. There are 4 user roles, namely Admin, HRD, Manager, and Members. The four roles have different menu access, depending on the level of the role they have. The features available in it are quite diverse, including:

  • Displays a list of all members who are having birthdays
  • Create certificate
  • Training assessment
  • Make a training report
  • Management members
  • Management training
  • Management position
  • Management department
  • Management module
